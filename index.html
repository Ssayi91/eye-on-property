Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our property and design services

How do I start the process of buying a property?

Starting your property journey begins with a consultation where we understand your needs, budget, and preferences. We then guide you through available options and help you make informed decisions.

What areas do you serve for property development?

We primarily focus on the Mombasa region, including Nyali, Bamburi, Kisauni, and surrounding areas. We also have projects in other developing regions across Kenya.

Do you offer financing options for property purchases?

Yes, we partner with several financial institutions to provide financing options for our clients. We can help you navigate mortgage options and other financing solutions.

How long does the property buying process take?

The timeline varies depending on the property type and location, but typically ranges from 30 to 90 days from initial consultation to closing.

How long does a typical interior design project take?

The timeline varies based on project scope. A single room may take 2-4 weeks, while a full home interior design project can take 8-12 weeks from concept to completion.

Do you offer custom furniture design?

Yes, we offer custom furniture design services tailored to your space, needs, and aesthetic preferences. Our team works with skilled craftsmen to create unique pieces.

Can you work with my existing furniture?

Absolutely! We specialize in incorporating existing pieces with new designs to create a cohesive look that respects your preferences and budget.

Do you provide 3D renderings of design concepts?

Yes, we provide detailed 3D renderings to help you visualize the final design before implementation. This ensures we're aligned on the vision before any work begins.
